# Public Assets for New Nomads Students

Always download your final images, logos, and icons from Figma and place them in this directory.

Do not hotlink Figma image URLs in production code.

Recommended structure:
- images/: export JPG/PNG/WebP screenshots and photos
- icons/: export SVG icon files
- logos/: export brand marks and lockups

Why local assets matter:
- Figma URLs can expire or require auth
- Faster and more stable deployments
- Better control over optimization and caching
